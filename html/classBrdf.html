Detailed Description

Brdf combines lambertian, metal materials and fresnel effect.

Brdf stands for bidirectional reflectance distribution function. The brdf defines how rays are scattered, just like lambertian or metal material does. This combines metal, lambertian materials and fresnel using some probability distribution.
