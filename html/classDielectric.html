Detailed Description

Dielectric material allows rays to pass through objects. It can simulate materials like water or glass.

When ray enters object, it is refracted or reflected according to snells law. The material also computes fresnel with Christhophe Schlick's polynomial approximation. Fresnel is: when ray hit's object in shallow angle, it is more likely to reflect than refract. When ray hits in steep angle it is more likely to refract than reflect.
