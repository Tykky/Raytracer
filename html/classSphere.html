Computes solution for Ray - Sphere hit equation.

hit equation for sphere: dot((Ray(c) - center),(Ray(c) - center))=R^2 (R = radius) solve for C. Ray function is Ray(c) = origin + c*direction, origin,direction are R^3 vectors.
